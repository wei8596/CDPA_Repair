FAQ
  • My network is broken! I can't get on the Internet! What should I do? 
First, please cross-test to see if there is something wrong with your PC, network cable, or jack.
  • I cross-tested. And I assume that the DVO (data/voice outlets) is broken! Could you help me? 
Feel free to connect to our website (CDPA Repair), and request a ticket on our reporting system.

HowTo

Step 1

Please search your MAC address on our banned list to check if you are blocked.

Step 2

Connect your PC to your roommate's DVO (which works), and see if you can get online.

Step 3

Connect your roommate's PC (which works) to your DVO, and see if you can get online.

Explanation

  • I found that I'm blocked in Step 1! 
According to why you're blocked, follow the instructions in the result page.
After complete the instructions, request a ticket on our reporting system (CDPA Repair).
  • "No" in Step 2, and "Yes" in Step 3 
There might be something wrong with your network configuration.

  • "Yes" in Step 2, and "No" in Step 3 
There could be something wrong with your DVO.
Please create an entry on our reporting system (CDPA Repair) along with the result of cross-test.
